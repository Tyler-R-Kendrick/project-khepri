# Code Of Conduct

Project Khepri contributors are expected to keep collaboration respectful, direct, and evidence-oriented.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent while still naming risks clearly.
- Use reproducible evidence for technical claims.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Respect privacy and do not share secrets or private transcripts.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Intentionally disruptive conduct.
- Dismissing security or safety concerns without investigation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block participation, or take other reasonable action to protect the project and contributors.
